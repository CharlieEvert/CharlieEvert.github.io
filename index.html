About Me

I'm Charlie! I am from St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands and I made the US mainland my home from the age of 14. My time as a paratrooper in the US Army taught me resilience, but an injury led me to chart a different path. This journey introduced me to the fascinating world of Generative AI and Data Science, where I've been fortunate to contribute, learn, and meet some awesome folks.

With academic foundations from Temple University and Saint Joseph's University, I've been able to delve deep into the field. Today, I serve as an AI & Data Engineering Consultant at Deloitte. Beyond my professional commitments, I find purpose in volunteering with organizations like Hire Heroes USA and Statistics Without Borders.

Previously, I built the Generative AI Platform at Kenvue and pioneered many use cases involving dynamic dashboarding, structured data querying with natural language, guided document generation and traditional RAG with semantic search. I have worked with the OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, MSFT, Pinecone & Databricks Gen AI teams to implement their platforms/tools into a variety of use cases, and I am always looking to learn new things and meet cool people!

For a deep dive into my latest work in Generative AI, check out the projects below. These embody the cutting-edge of my explorations and achievements in the field.
