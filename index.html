About Me

Hi, I’m Charlie Evert, an AI & Data Engineering Consultant, author, and tech enthusiast. I’ve deployed Generative AI, data, and analytics applications at scale—serving up to 1 million monthly users—as a technical lead. I grew up in St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands, served as an infantry paratrooper, and eventually found my way to tech. I enjoy leading teams to build cutting edge, art of the possible apps in record time and then putting those into production.

I love creating—whether it’s writing children’s books, publishing research, developing iOS apps, or tackling new projects. When I’m not building, my Westie and Maltese keep me company.

Check out my latest work and thoughts on my YouTube channel, Prompt Hub, where I explore Generative AI and other tech curiosities. See below for my publications, projects and other passions.
