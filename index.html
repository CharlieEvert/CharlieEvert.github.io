A bit about me

I grew up in St. Thomas in the US Virgin Islands, and have lived in the mainland USA since I was 14 years old. I have been honorably discharged from the US Army for a broken shoulder (from a fast roping accident), and have since discovered my passion for creating Generative AI Applications & Data Science solutions at the cutting edge of human knowledge.

I graduated from Temple University's BBA in Accounting program, and received both an MBA & MS in Business Intelligence and Analytics specializing in Data Science from Saint Joseph's University during the COVID-19 pandemic. I am currently working as a Sr. Product Innovation Analyst & Generative AI/Data Engineer at Kenvue (formerly Johnson & Johnson). I enjoy volunteering with Hire Heroes USA and Statistics Without Borders- it's awesome to see data make a difference in the world. I enjoy looking for the perfect milkshake (Black Tap in NYC is the top for now), hiking, hanging with my dogs and learning new things!

At Kenvue, in November of 2022 I became enamored with the OpenAI API and built out some impactful Retrieval Augmented Generation based applications with internal data. Given the success of these, I reached out directly to OpenAI. When I met with the folks there, my career shifted from data engineering/science to innovating on the frontier of human knowledge and it has been an amazing journey. Since then, I have identified significant cost saving measures, processed embeddings at hyper-scale and solved dozens of Generative AI use cases on top of building the Kenvue Generative AI Platform. I have had the awesome opportunity to collaborate with teams at Microsoft, Google & Pinecone in addition to OpenAI and the work my team was able to do has consistently been at the cutting edge. I have trained many teams as an SME, and have made it a point to pass on the experience. I want to continue to pioneer this new field of Generative AI Engineering; to me it represents a tremendous opportunity to automate mundane work and grant power to people to focus on less repetitive + more impactful pursuits.
